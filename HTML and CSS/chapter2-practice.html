The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly has been starring out of her window for an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist and all the other scientific journals her work had appeared in, was a well-thumbed copy of On The Road. It has been Molly's favourite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she feels free

She had spend the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster quote proclaiming that Work is the refugee of people who had no better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unravelling the screte of the DNA, to be an outstanding achievement. Molly did think she had something better to do.
